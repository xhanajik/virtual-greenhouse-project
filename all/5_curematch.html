CureMatch

webpage link

CureMatch Bionov is a HIPAA and GDPR-compliant web-based CDSS platform focused on personalized medicine, which provides oncological combination therapies based on entire molecular profiles. Cure- Match also considers other patient characteristics besides molecular data, for example, comorbidities, medical history, or previous treatment lines [1].

Approach
CureMatch takes an annotated patient report in PDF form. Its approach involves ranking the alteration and treatments to implement combination therapy [1]. One of the CDSS’s strengths is that it regards complete molecular profiles rather than targeting individual alterations [1]. Each recommendation has a ’matching score’ which the CDSS uses to pick the top three 3-drug and 2-drug combination therapies and monotherapies [2]. Likewise, it provides clinical trial information as evidence for the suggested combinations.

Usage
The vendor estimates the time for report generation to be 2-3 days [1]. Besides general information, the report includes the number of pathogenic and actionable markers, matching drugs, relevant combinations, and all considered combinations. It also contains the treatments ’match score’ and the top three drugs or combinations for each category [2].
