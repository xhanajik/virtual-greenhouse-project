Summary

Table illustrating concordance between identified CDSSs and the obtained requirements:

This table contains the general information about CDSSs identified in the review and the sources from which data shown in the table were extracted. It also illustrates, whether or not the identified CDSSs reflect a specific set of requirements defined by a domain expert. Green means that the CDSS fulfills the requirement to some degree. On the other hand, red cells signify that the CDSS does not implement the desired functionality. The special cases (yellow cells) signify, that the CDSS was intended to support patient-doctor discussions of treatment benefits and side-effects during consultations. All websites were accessed on 1 December 2022.
